# Resonance

Take control of your combat audio. Resonance lets you mute and replace spell sounds, silence combat grunts, quiet creature noises, and remove weapon clang — all without touching your game files.

## Features

### Class Sound Presets
Built-in presets for every class with hand-picked replacements that swap overly loud or repetitive modern spell sounds with subtler classic alternatives. One click to apply, and they stay up to date as presets improve.

### Per-Spell Sound Control
Configure any spell individually. Pick a replacement sound from thousands of in-game audio files, or mute a spell entirely. Works with any spell you can cast.

### Creature Vocalization Muting
Significantly reduce ambient monster sounds — attack grunts, injury cries, death screams, and aggro roars — by creature category (Beast, Demon, Dragonkin, Elemental, Humanoid, Mechanical, Undead). Great for keeping dungeon and raid audio less chaotic.

### Character Vocalization Muting
Silence your own combat exertion sounds (grunts, shouts, battle cries) or mute all player races at once.

### Weapon Impact Muting
Remove the melee hit sounds (the constant thwack/clang on every auto-attack) across all weapon types.

### Full Customization
- **Manual FID muting** — mute any specific sound file in the game by FileDataID
- **Sound channel routing** — play replacement sounds through Master, SFX, Music, or Ambience
- **Import/Export** — share your configurations with other players via preset strings
- **Save presets** — save your current setup as a named preset and swap between configurations

## Getting Started

1. Install the addon and `/reload`
2. Open settings: **Esc > Options > AddOns > Resonance** (or type `/res`)
3. Go to the **Presets** tab and apply your class preset
4. Tweak individual spells in the **Spells** tab if you want

## Slash Commands

- `/res` — Open the options panel
- `/res toggle` — Enable/disable the addon
- `/res debug` — Toggle debug mode (prints spell IDs on cast)
